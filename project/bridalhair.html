Bridal Hair Accesory

Description

The Inspirations are taken from the head gears, craft technique, flora and fauna of the Tangkhul Naga tribes of Manipur. Handicraft is closely associated with self and souls of Tribal's. The people are artistic and imaginative , different designs and styles of the original head gears were imitated from the birds and animals that ancestors see. The materials used for all the products are sustainable which includes bamboo, cane stick, beads, feathers, woolen threads, etc.
