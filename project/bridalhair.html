Bridal Hair Accesory

Description

This project centres around the theme of boldness and elegance that women embody. It was inspired by the head gears, craft technique, flora and fauna of the Tangkhul Naga tribes of Manipur. Handicraft is very integral to the culture and lifestyle of the Tangkhul Nagas. The people are artistic and imaginative. Different designs and styles of the original head gears were imitated from the birds and animals that the ancestors associate with. I adopted some of these designs by using materials that are sustainable which includes bamboo, cane stick, beads, feathers, woollen threads, etc for all the products.
